---
title: "Fluffy Pancakes"
image: "/recipix/images/pancakes.jpg"
description: "Simple recipe for fluffy pancakes, perfect for breakfast."
ingredients:
  - 200g flour
  - 300ml milk
  - 2 eggs
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - pinch of salt
instructions:
  - Mix flour with baking powder and salt.
  - Beat the eggs with milk and pour over dry ingredients.
  - Cook pancakes in a hot pan.
---
