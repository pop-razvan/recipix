Fluffy Pancakes

Simple recipe for fluffy pancakes, perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 300ml milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour with baking powder and salt.
  2. Beat the eggs with milk and pour over dry ingredients.
  3. Cook pancakes in a hot pan.
